FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get all your painting questions answered. Browse the most asked questions, information on products, services, and painting projects or reach out for personalized support.

Do you do colour mixing?

Yes indeed we do. We offer professional color mixing services to match any shade you desire. Visit one of our stores with a sample for accurate color matching.

What's the price for paint?

Kindly call the number provided for more information. You can also get a rough estimate of your price by using the paint calculator. But for a more customized service call the line and one of our experts will be able to assist you.

Can I buy online?

Yes you can. Visit our online store to check out the products you can buy online now. We also offer deliveries within Lusaka at a small fee.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within Lusaka takes 1-2 business days. For other parts of Zambia, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on the location.

Do you offer painting services?

Yes, we offer professional painting consultation and application services. Our certified painters can help bring your vision to life with our premium paint products.
