Products & Colors

Do you do custom color mixing?

Yes, we offer professional color mixing services to match any shade you desire. Bring in a sample or color reference to one of our stores, and our experts will create the perfect match for your project.

What's the difference between your paint ranges?

Our DuraCoat range offers premium quality for both interior and exterior use with excellent coverage. WeatherTex is specially formulated for exterior applications with enhanced weather resistance. WallCoat is designed for interior walls and ceilings with a smooth finish.

How long does paint last once opened?

When stored properly in a cool, dry place with the lid tightly sealed, unopened paint can last 2-5 years. Once opened, latex paint typically lasts 2-3 years while oil-based paint can last up to 15 years if stored correctly.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. You can securely complete your payment during checkout on our website or at any of our retail locations.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within Lusaka takes 1-2 business days. For other parts of Zambia, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on the location. Express delivery options are available for an additional fee.

Can I return unused paint?

Due to the nature of paint products, we generally do not accept returns for custom-mixed colors or opened containers. However, we may make exceptions for defective products or delivery errors. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

Application & Technical

How much area does a liter of paint cover?

Generally, 1 liter of our interior paints covers approximately 10-12 square meters per coat on smooth surfaces. Coverage may vary depending on the texture of the surface, application method, and the specific product used.

Do I need to prime before painting?

Priming is recommended for new surfaces, previously unpainted wood, and when making drastic color changes. Our WallCoat Plaster Primer is specifically designed for new plaster surfaces to ensure optimal adhesion and coverage.

How do I prepare surfaces before painting?

Proper surface preparation is crucial for lasting results. Clean the surface to remove dirt, grease, and loose paint. Sand glossy surfaces to create texture for better adhesion. Fill cracks and holes with appropriate filler. Apply primer if needed before the topcoat.
